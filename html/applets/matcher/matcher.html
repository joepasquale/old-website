Stable Matching Solver

The Stable Matching Solver is an algorithmic solution to the Stable Marriage Problem, which is the problem of finding a stable matching between two equally sized sets of elements given an ordering of preferences for each element. Simply add the appropriate members to the preferred set, and the rest of the members to the regular set. If the sets are equally sized, then the matcher will return the optimal matching based on the Gale-Shapely Algorithm
